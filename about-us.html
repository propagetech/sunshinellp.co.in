ABOUT US

Sunshine  Mining & Crushing Solutions LLP

Established in the year 2017 with the 4 promoters having the specialized skills in Planning, Mining & Crushing, Logistics, Statutory fulfilment and accounting. Sunshine is the complete solution provider of your needs.

We nurture ethical business values and empower talent pools to create, develop & sustain newer business models for greater community development

ANY Mine ANY Minerals you mine, we deliver quality services across the entire life cycle of mining projects from front-end geology, processing, quality and environmental consultation, through to conceptual and detailed design, project management, construction, operations, and mine closure. Our concept-to-closure capabilities provide continuity of support and knowledge synergy across the project phases.

Sunshine Mining & Crushing Solutions LLP expertize in the field of mining and crushing and trading of M.Sand & Plastering Sand, an industry-recognized expert in every facet of mining project assessment, development and optimization for all major commodities. In the early stages of project development, we utilize our skills in geology, mineral resource estimation, mine planning, and metallurgical studies to add significant value to our customers' projects. We optimize the connection between mineral resource estimates and the proposed mining method to maximize cash flow in early face of mining, increase net present value, and develop efficient flow sheets that maximize commodity recovery and reduce contaminants, and minimize plant footprint and complexity to reduce capital expenditures.

In addition, we provide full support of reporting requirements for public disclosure, from preliminary economic assessments through to detailed feasibility studies.


At the detailed engineering stage, our relentless focus on the capital efficiency of the design realizes substantial cost savings. Automation and technology- based material handling solutions for capacities greater than 10,000 tonnes per day, including our proprietary systems are proven to increase productivity and efficiency.

Our systematic, scalable, globally proven approach to project delivery, combined with our specialized technical expertise and strong project management capability, reduces project risk and shortens execution. From small expansions to new builds, our experts work in concert across the life cycle to consistently deliver projects safely and predictably, regardless of size, complexity or location.

Our areas of expertise include:
  • Front-end services, including exploration assistance, resource, and reserve estimates, advanced geo statistics, mine planning and design, geotechnical and environmental services, and scoping pre-feasibility and feasibility studies
  • Project, logistics and construction management
  • Engineering, procurement and construction management (EPCM), engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC), and Project Management Contractor (PMC)
  • Annual Maintenance contract in crusher plants & producing of M.Sand & Aggregates.
  • Plant startup and commissioning.
  • Finalization of plant Flow Sheets & Errection of Crusher Plant.
  • Material trading (Aggregates, M.Sand & P.Sand)
  • Logistics arrangement service
  • Mine closure and rehabilitation
