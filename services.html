M.Sand / P.Sand product approval from PWD, Tamil Nadu & setting of Laboratory facilities at work site

New M.Sand policy 2023 released by the Tamil Nadu government on 09.03.2023 as per new policy it is compulsory to get the M.Sand and P.Sand product approval from the Public Works Department ( PWD) for producing the M.Sand / Concrete Sand and to setup of Testing laboratory is mandatory at work site.

We are doing to get the approval from the PWD and setting of lab facilities at work site with cost effective manner.
